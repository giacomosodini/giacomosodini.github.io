Welcome! I am a mathematician working on Optimal Transport, Non-Smooth Analysis, and Calculus of Variations. Currently I am a PostDoc in the research group of Ulisse Stefanelli at the Faculty of Mathematics of the University of Vienna.

Until September 2022, I was a PhD student under the supervision of Giuseppe Savaré at TU Munich in the group of Massimo Fornasier. Previously, I completed my bachelor and master studies at Politecnico di Milano, advised by Sandro Salsa and Luigi Ambrosio.
